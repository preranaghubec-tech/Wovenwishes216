Hello, I'm

I'm a self-taught hobby artist who has loved drawing, painting, and creating things by hand ever since I was a child. Over the years, I discovered that the most meaningful art isn't just something beautiful—it's something personal.

What started as handmade gifts for my family and friends slowly grew into creating personalized pieces for others. I love understanding the memories, stories, and little details that mean the most to someone and turning them into something they can hold onto forever.

Every piece I make is one of a kind, created with love, care, and a personal touch. My hope is that each creation brings joy and becomes a cherished part of someone's story.

My story

How it all started

It started with a sketchbook and some coloured pencils my mum gave me years ago. I'd always doodled, but one day I sat down and actually finished something — and the feeling was indescribable. Over the years, art became much more than a hobby. During difficult times, painting helped me hold on, slow down, and find joy again. I've also always been a dreamer and a traveller at heart. I would paint places I longed to visit—seashores, gardens, campfires, and cozy little corners of the world. Somehow, many of those paintings later became real memories, which made me believe a little in the magic of manifestation. Since then, I haven't stopped.

Today I work across paintings, textile art, and paper craft. I love that each medium tells a different kind of story. Paintings are where I dream. Paper craft is where I play.

I'm based in Hyderabad and I ship all over India. If you'd like something custom — a particular colour, a specific size, a gift for someone special — just drop me an email. I love custom work.
